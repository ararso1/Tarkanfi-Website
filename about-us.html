About Us

Tarkanfi General Cooperative

Tarkanfi is a cooperative of over 400 dedicated farmers working together to promote and distribute high-quality agricultural products. We serve both local markets and international buyers, ensuring a reliable supply of fresh, organic goods from farm to table.

Our mission is to empower rural communities through sustainable agriculture and fair trade, while also enhancing the livelihoods of our members.

Empowering Rural Agriculture

Tarkanfi is committed to supporting its members with modern agricultural tools, improved seeds, and farming inputs. We import essential equipment that boosts productivity and supports year-round farming across diverse environments.

With strong internal systems and collective strength, we ensure transparency, fair pricing, and economic opportunity for our farmers.

Farming Innovation & Market Impact

From quality produce like coffee, khat, teff, and honey to livestock such as goats and camels, our cooperative brings the best of Ethiopian agriculture to domestic and global markets.

We also help farmers access irrigation pumps, fertilizers, and advanced tools that reduce labor and increase yield.
