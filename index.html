Our Services

Reliable Agricultural Distribution

Tarkanfi facilitates the collection, quality control, and distribution of fresh produce to local and international markets.

Essential Farming Tools & Inputs

We import and supply high-quality tools, fertilizers, and farming inputs to enhance productivity and reduce costs for our members.

Built for Smallholders & Large Buyers

Our cooperative serves both smallholder farmers and commercial buyers through transparent, efficient, and scalable services.

Market-Ready Agricultural Solutions

We ensure our products meet rigorous quality standards and are well-prepared for retail, wholesale, and export markets.

Responsive Support Team

Custom Partnerships Available
